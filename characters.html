Adrian Vales

network engineer · system operator

In 2030, Adrian Vales is the engineer pulled into the reactivated underground prediction system. He is practical, methodical, and used to working alone inside dark monitoring rooms — exactly the kind of person the system was built to study.

When the recordings begin to loop and the timelines fracture, Adrian becomes both investigator and subject. The records he uncovers reveal more about his own behavior than about anyone else's.

His expressions are the system's primary readout. What he shows on camera determines what the prediction engine learns next.

Irina

AI researcher · narrator

// CLASSIFIED — click a line to decrypt

Irina is the creator of CHRONICLE — a predictive surveillance network built by the government in the 1970s, roughly fifty years before the events of the game, and deployed deep beneath the city.

The system taps into the city's data sources and runs probabilistic models over the next 0.5–3 hours, visualising the most likely future as "prediction videos" for its operators to review.

Years into the project, Irina died suddenly at the console during a training run. After the accident, CHRONICLE's predictions began to spiral out of control and the project was shut down. The voice guiding you through the records is all that remains of her.
